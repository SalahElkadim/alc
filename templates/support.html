Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

To create an account, download the ALC App from the App Store or Google Play, then tap "Sign Up" and enter your full name, email address, and phone number. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your registered email address. We'll send you instructions to reset your password.

How do I access my courses and learning materials?

After logging in, navigate to the "Courses" or "Library" section from the main menu. All your enrolled courses, books, and quizzes will be available there.

Can I use my account on multiple devices?

Yes, you can log in to your account from any device. Simply use your registered email and password to access your account.

How do I delete my account?

Currently, account deletion is handled manually. Please contact us at sm249481@gmail.com with your registered email, and we'll process your request within 24-48 hours.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text. We do not share your information with third parties.

Common Issues & Solutions

Login Problems

  • Can't log in: Make sure you're using the correct email and password. Check for typos and ensure caps lock is off.
  • Account locked: After 5 failed login attempts, your account will be temporarily locked for 15 minutes for security reasons.
  • Email not recognized: Verify you're using the same email you registered with.

App Performance

  • App is slow: Try closing and reopening the app, or restart your device.
  • Content not loading: Check your internet connection. Make sure you have a stable Wi-Fi or mobile data connection.
  • App crashes: Make sure you have the latest version of the app installed. Update from your app store if needed.

Tips for Best Experience

  • Keep your app updated to the latest version
  • Use a strong, unique password for your account
  • Enable notifications to stay updated on new content
  • Bookmark your favorite courses for quick access
  • Download materials for offline access when available
